# Introduction

Welcome to **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics: From Simulated Brains to Embodied Intelligence**.

This comprehensive textbook guides you through the fundamentals of physical AI and humanoid robotics over a 13-week course covering 21 chapters.

## What You'll Learn

- **Module 0**: Foundations of robotics, mathematics, and simulation
- **Module 1**: ROS 2 fundamentals for robot control
- **Module 2**: Digital twin development and simulation environments
- **Module 3**: NVIDIA Isaac Sim for AI-powered robotics
- **Module 4**: Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models for embodied AI
- **Capstone**: Build an integrated autonomous humanoid system

## Prerequisites

- Basic programming knowledge (Python recommended)
- Linear algebra and calculus fundamentals
- Familiarity with command-line interfaces

## How to Use This Textbook

Each chapter follows a structured 12-section format:

1. Learning Objectives
2. Prerequisites
3. Weekly Mapping
4. Motivating Scenario
5. Core Theory & Mathematics
6. Worked Example
7. Hands-On Code
8. Application to Humanoid Robots
9. Common Pitfalls & Debugging
10. Exercises
11. Further Reading & References

All code examples are runnable, version-pinned, and validated in CI environments.
